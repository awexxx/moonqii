Moonqii (Nathan King) was born on December 22, 2005 [Aged 13] in a small, rural community in Western New York.
Moon never thought of being a YouTuber; he had actually no idea at all of what he wanted to be. In 2018, he tried out making videos and actually liked the concept.
In 2019, his channel had an explosion of subscribers and views.
In 2020, he hit 1,000 subscribers. As of January 2021, Moonqii has 1.6k concurrent subscribers.
